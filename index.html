About KoshurBagh

KoshurBagh is an innovative agriculture platform dedicated to supporting farmers in Kashmir. We aim to modernize orchard management, improve productivity, and provide easy access to vital farming information.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower local Kashmiri farmers with technology to optimize their orchards, track harvests, and make informed decisions that boost yield and income.

Why KoshurBagh?

  • Organize your orchards with photos, fruit types (Apple, Walnut, etc.), and status tracking.
  • Maintain harvest logs including date, quantity, notes, and optional images for better record-keeping.
  • Get real-time notifications about approvals, suggestions, and important updates.
  • Personalized farmer dashboard with greetings and weather updates to plan daily tasks efficiently.
  • Easy profile management including farmer name, contact details, and orchard information.

Our Vision

We envision a connected Kashmiri farming community where technology simplifies orchard management and improves overall farm productivity, helping farmers focus on what they do best—growing quality produce.

Join Us

By joining KoshurBagh, you become part of a modern, efficient, and sustainable farming ecosystem. Together, we can grow better orchards, record accurate harvests, and improve livelihoods in Kashmir.
