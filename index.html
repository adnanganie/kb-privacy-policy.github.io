Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is important to us. KoshurBagh collects personal and orchard-related information to enhance your farming experience, provide weather updates, send notifications, and help manage your orchards efficiently.

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. All data is securely stored and used solely for improving app functionality and farmer support.

By using KoshurBagh, you consent to our collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Data We Collect

  • Farmer details: name, contact number, and address.
  • Orchard information: name, location, fruit type, and images.
  • Harvest logs: date, quantity, notes, and optional images.
  • App usage data for improving features and services.

Your Rights

  • You can update or delete your personal and orchard information anytime.
  • You can opt out of receiving notifications.
  • We respect your right to privacy and comply with all applicable data protection regulations.

For questions or concerns regarding privacy, please contact us at support@koshurbagh.com.
